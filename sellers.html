SELLERS

Seller guidance designed to help your home stand out

A thoughtful sales strategy starts with preparation. This page gives Connecticut homeowners a polished starting point before listing, pricing, and preparing their home for the market.

Start with presentation

  • Declutter surfaces, closets, and open living areas.
  • Deep clean the home so it feels bright and well maintained.
  • Touch up paint, fixtures, and smaller cosmetic details.

Improve curb appeal

  • Make the exterior feel welcoming and neat.
  • Trim landscaping and refresh the entry if needed.
  • Remember that first impressions begin before buyers step inside.

Price strategically

  • Pricing affects attention, showings, and offer activity.
  • A smart pricing strategy can create stronger momentum from the beginning.
  • Local guidance matters when positioning your home in the market.

Showcase the home well

  • Professional photos and polished marketing matter.
  • Each room should feel clean, open, and easy to imagine living in.
  • Simple staging touches can make a big difference.

SELLER STRATEGY

Helpful reminders before you go live

  • Focus on the overall buyer experience, not just the house itself.
  • Be open to feedback that can improve the home’s presentation.
  • Prepare emotionally for showings, questions, and negotiation.
  • Trust a thoughtful process built around strategy and professionalism.
