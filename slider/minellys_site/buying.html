BUYING IN CONNECTICUT

Start your home search with clarity and confidence

Buying a home can feel exciting and overwhelming at the same time. This page helps buyers understand the basics before they begin.

FIRST STEPS

How to get ready before you shop

1. Review your budget

  • Look at your monthly comfort range, not just the maximum loan amount.
  • Plan for utilities, insurance, taxes, and future maintenance.
  • Think through what works best for your lifestyle and long-term goals.

2. Get pre-approved

  • Speak with a trusted lender early in the process.
  • Learn how much you may qualify for and what payment range makes sense.
  • Pre-approval helps strengthen your offer when you find the right home.

3. Make your wish list

  • Separate must-haves from nice-to-haves.
  • Think about location, commute, school preferences, parking, and layout.
  • Keep your priorities realistic and flexible.

4. Understand upfront costs

  • Be ready for your down payment and closing costs.
  • Ask questions about inspections, appraisals, and lender fees.
  • Building a clear plan early can reduce stress later.

BUYER TIPS

Ways to shop smart

  • Move quickly when you see a property that fits your goals.
  • Stay focused on your budget and avoid stretching too far emotionally.
  • Ask questions about condition, timeline, and neighborhood factors.
  • Keep your documents organized so you are ready when it matters most.
  • Work with an agent who will guide you honestly through each decision.
