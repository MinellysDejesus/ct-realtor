RENTALS

Get prepared before you apply

Finding the right rental is easier when you are organized, responsive, and ready with the documents landlords often want to see.

RENTAL PREP

Helpful items to have ready

Documents

  • Government-issued ID
  • Recent pay stubs or proof of income
  • References and prior landlord information
  • Funds prepared for application and move-in costs

Smart search habits

  • Be clear about your budget and ideal location.
  • Respond quickly when you see a good fit.
  • Ask questions about utilities, parking, and lease terms.
  • Keep your must-haves realistic.

How to stand out

  • Be polite, responsive, and prepared.
  • Submit a complete application when possible.
  • Have your supporting documents ready to send promptly.
  • Show reliability and professionalism in communication.

Avoid common problems

  • Be cautious of listings that seem unusually cheap.
  • Verify property details before sending money.
  • Read lease terms carefully before signing.
  • Ask questions if something feels unclear.
