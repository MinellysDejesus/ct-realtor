ABOUT MINELLYS

Driven by purpose, grounded in faith, and built for real connection.

Minellys Dejesus brings a thoughtful, elevated, and deeply personal approach to real estate across Connecticut—helping clients feel supported, informed, and confident from the first conversation to closing day.

HER STORY

She didn’t follow the traditional path—and that’s exactly what makes her different.

With a background in business administration, Minellys had every opportunity to step into a corporate career. But she knew early on that a desk job and climbing a corporate ladder was not the life she wanted. She was searching for something more—something natural, meaningful, and aligned with who she truly is.

Real estate wasn’t always the plan. Growing up, she was surrounded by it through her father, an independent contractor who worked on homes. What once felt like background noise slowly became something more. Over time, her curiosity grew—and one day, it simply clicked.

Why not try it?

That decision changed everything. What started as curiosity quickly turned into passion. With her natural familiarity with homes, strong work ethic, and ability to connect with people, real estate became more than a career—it became her purpose.

MORE THAN JUST REAL ESTATE

For Minellys, it’s never just about the transaction.

She takes time to truly understand each client—their needs, their lifestyle, and what home means to them. Whether she’s helping a family purchase their first home or guiding an investor through their next move, she leads with care, attention, and intention.

Her clients often describe her as responsive, driven, and caring. She believes in being honest, transparent, and fully present throughout the process. No shortcuts. No pressure. Just real guidance and real results.

BUILT ON GROWTH, FAITH & CONNECTION

The values behind the experience she creates

Personal growth

Real estate helped Minellys discover her strength, intelligence, and attention to detail. Today she shows up with confidence, humility, and a commitment to keep learning.

Faith & trust

One of her most meaningful client experiences was helping a couple find their home right before their wedding—a journey grounded in trust, connection, and faith.

Intentional service

She doesn’t believe in doing the bare minimum. Every recommendation, follow-up, and strategy is built around helping clients feel seen, supported, and sure of their decisions.

ROOTED IN CONNECTICUT

Local insight with real appreciation for every corner of the state

Born and raised in Connecticut, Minellys has a deep appreciation for what makes each community special—from the charm of Fairfield County to the energy and creativity of New Haven, especially East Rock, Westville, and Wooster Square.

To her, Connecticut offers the perfect balance: peaceful living with easy access to major cities like New York and Boston. It’s not just where she works—it’s home.

BEYOND REAL ESTATE

A disciplined, creative, and heart-led approach to life and business

Outside of her work, Minellys is deeply focused on becoming the best version of herself. She values time with family, enjoys photography and creative expression, and stays committed to her fitness and personal growth journey.

Her discipline, consistency, and motivation carry over into how she serves her clients every day. The same care she puts into her own growth is the care she brings into every home search, sale, and client relationship.

Comfort. Peace. Confidence.

That’s what she wants every client to feel when they find the place that’s meant for them.

HER PROMISE

She’s here to listen, guide, and go the extra mile.

Because to Minellys, home is more than just a place. It’s comfort. It’s peace. It’s where life happens. Helping people find that is what she’s truly passionate about.
