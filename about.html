ABOUT MINELLYS

Driven by purpose, grounded in faith, and built on real connection.

Minellys Dejesus brings a thoughtful, elevated, and deeply personal approach to real estate across Connecticut—helping buyers, sellers, and renters feel supported, informed, and confident from the first conversation to closing day.

HER STORY

She didn’t follow the traditional path—and that’s exactly what makes her different.

With a background in business administration, she had every opportunity to step into a corporate career. But she knew early on that a desk job and climbing a corporate ladder wasn’t the life she wanted. She was looking for something more—something that felt natural, meaningful, and aligned with who she is.

Real estate wasn’t always the plan. Growing up, she was surrounded by it through her father, an independent contractor who worked on homes. What once felt like background noise slowly became something more. Over time, her curiosity grew—and one day, it simply clicked.

Why not try it?

That decision changed everything. What started as curiosity quickly turned into passion. With her natural familiarity with homes, strong work ethic, and ability to connect with people, real estate became more than a career—it became her purpose.

MORE THAN JUST REAL ESTATE

For Minellys, it’s never just about the transaction.

She takes the time to truly understand each client—their needs, their lifestyle, and what “home” means to them. Whether she’s helping a first-time buyer, a growing family, or an investor planning their next move, she approaches every situation with care, attention, and intention.

Her clients often describe her as responsive, driven, and genuinely caring—and that shows in everything she does. She believes in being honest, transparent, and fully present throughout the process. No shortcuts. No pressure. Just real guidance and real results.

BUILT ON GROWTH, FAITH & CONNECTION

The values behind the experience she creates

Personal growth

There was a time when she doubted herself—questioning her abilities and whether she was capable of succeeding in something that required so much responsibility. But through real estate, she discovered her strength, her intelligence, and her attention to detail.

Faith & connection

One of her most meaningful experiences was helping a couple find their home right before their wedding—a journey that went beyond real estate and became something personal, grounded in trust and faith.

Intentional service

She shows up every day ready to improve, learn, and give her clients her absolute best. Every recommendation, follow-up, and strategy is built around helping clients feel seen, supported, and confident.

ROOTED IN CONNECTICUT

Local insight with a real appreciation for every corner of the state.

Born and raised in Connecticut, Minellys has a deep appreciation for everything the state has to offer. From the charm of Fairfield County to the energy and creativity of New Haven—especially East Rock, Westville, and Wooster Square—she understands the unique feel of each community.

To her, Connecticut offers the perfect balance: peaceful living with easy access to major cities like New York and Boston. It’s not just where she works—it’s home.

BEYOND REAL ESTATE

A disciplined, creative, and heart-led approach to life and business.

Outside of her work, Minellys is deeply focused on becoming the best version of herself. She values time with family, enjoys photography and creative expression, and stays committed to her fitness and personal growth journey.

Her discipline, consistency, and motivation carry over into how she serves her clients every day. The same care she puts into her own growth is the care she brings into every home search, sale, and client relationship.

Comfort. Peace. Confidence.

That’s what she wants every client to feel when they find the place that’s meant for them.

HER PROMISE

She’s here to listen, guide, and go the extra mile.

Minellys isn’t here to do the bare minimum. She’s here to help every client feel seen, supported, and confident in their decisions. Because to her, home is more than just a place—it’s comfort, peace, and where life happens.
